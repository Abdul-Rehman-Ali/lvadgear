Frequently Asked Questions

What is an LVAD battery vest?

It’s specialized clothing with secure pockets that hold the LVAD controller and batteries firmly in place.

Can I wear the vest under regular clothes?

Absolutely. Many customers layer our vests under everyday outfits for a discreet, formal look.

How do I wash my LVAD vest safely?
